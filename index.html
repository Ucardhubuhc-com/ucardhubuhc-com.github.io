What is UnitedHealthcare (UHC)?

UCard is a versatile healthcare and benefits card given by UnitedHealthcare (UHC) at Ucardhub.uhc.com to make it simpler for members to use their health plan benefits, shop for eligible items, and pay for medical services and medical related things.Information of benefits, activation process, and how to use it.

UnitedHealthcare (UHC) is one of the largest healthcare insurance providers in the U.S. They provide various Medicare Advantage, Medicaid, and individual health plans to help millions access quality healthcare services, wellness programs, and prescription drug benefits.

What is the UCard?

UCard is a person-specific card issued by UnitedHealthcare that covers multiple health benefits in one card. Members can use UCard for Medicare Advantage, wellness programs, eligible healthcare purchases, and other medical services. The card can be managed online via Ucardhub.uhc.com .

How to Activate UCard?

Before using the UCard, it must be activated. Users can activate their card via Ucardhub.uhc.com , phone, or the mobile app.

Online Activation using activate.uhc.com

  1. Go to activate.uhc.com on a web browser
  2. Log in with your UnitedHealthcare account (or create one if you don’t have it)
  3. Enter the UCard details and follow the activation process
  4. Once activated, you can start using your UCard immediately

Activate UCard by Phone

  1. Call the UCard activation number found on the card
  2. Follow the instructions and enter your card details
  3. Wait for the confirmation message indicating successful activation

Activate via the UHC Mobile App

  1. Download the UnitedHealthcare app from the App Store or Google Play
  2. Log in to your UHC account (or create one if you don’t have it)
  3. Navigate to the UCard activation section and follow the instructions
  4. Once activated, manage and track usage within the app
